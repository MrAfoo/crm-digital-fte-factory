## Core Products

### 1. NovaDeskAI Platform
Our flagship helpdesk automation system that intelligently manages customer inquiries across multiple channels. Features include:
- Automated ticket routing and categorization
- AI-powered auto-tagging and ticket summarization
- Real-time SLA tracking and breach alerts
- Canned response libraries with AI suggestions
- Seamless agent-AI collaboration interface

### 2. NovaBot
A specialized AI chatbot trained on your knowledge base and customer data. Capabilities:
- Multi-channel deployment (Email, WhatsApp, Web)
- Context-aware responses with conversation memory
- Intelligent escalation to human agents
- Configurable tone and personality settings
- Custom escalation thresholds and fallback flows

### 3. NovaSight Analytics Dashboard
Advanced analytics and reporting system providing deep insights into customer support operations:
- Real-time metrics tracking and historical trends
- Custom report builder
- Team performance dashboards
- Customer sentiment analysis
- Integration with business intelligence tools
